SOMETHING SPICY

Where & When

Alden Bridge Sports Complex
The Woodlands, Texas
October 12-13, 2024

Team Price: $1500

Deposit: $500

Age: MS-HS

Games:

  • 21 minute games
  • 4 game minimum
  • No timeouts
  • Substitutions "on the fly" or during dead ball
  • Zero-Tolerance Policy on Fighting

Operation:

  • Balls on the end lines, always

Divisions:

  • This is a girls only event
  • Divisions and brackets may be adjusted based on participating teams to ensure the best competition for all registered teams

Schedule:

  • The game schedule will be posted the Monday preceding the event weekend
  • Teams will be placed in appropriate divisions based on age and then skill level (ex: HS A vs HS B)

Venue:

  • Free parking
  • Bathrooms on site
  • Dogs allowed (leashed)

Free Agents ($85 for registation)

  • Please register here to be a free agent player. We will create a team with other free agent players or add you to an existing club program that needs a player.
  • Email thesauceguy@txlhsl.com with any questions regarding Free Agent availability and registration.

Refunds:

If weather or other unforeseen circumstances prevent an event from being completed or in some cases, even starting, refunds will be as follows: 75% to teams that do not start any game and 50% to teams that compete 1 game. No refunds will be given to teams once they have completed 2 games.

Important:

All tournament participants must be current USA Lacrosse members in order to participate. US Lacrosse membership numbers will be collected and verified when completing tournament waivers. If you are not a current member, or your membership is set to expire during the event, please visit USA Lacrosse to register.

CANCELLATION/REFUND POLICY

If weather or other unforeseen circumstances prevent an event from being completed or in some case, even starting, refunds will be as follows: 75% to teams that do not start any game and 50% to teams that compete 1 game. No refunds will be given to teams once they have completed 2 games.

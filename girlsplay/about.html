ABOUT US

GirlsPlay Lax Tournaments was founded by Hot Sauce Lacrosse as an opportunity to give girls lacrosse players of all talent levels to play in more game scenarios and get experience. We wanted to focus on the growing sport of girls lacrosse specifically and cater to them.

GirlsPlay operates a few tournaments throughout the state of Texas with 20-30 teams attending annually.

CANCELLATION/REFUND POLICY

If weather or other unforeseen circumstances prevent an event from being completed or in some case, even starting, refunds will be as follows: 75% to teams that do not start any game and 50% to teams that compete 1 game. No refunds will be given to teams once they have completed 2 games.

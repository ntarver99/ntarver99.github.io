FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Are teams permitted to include players from the grade above or below on a roster if they are short on players for their own grade?

Players can play up but not down (i.e. players 2027 and below can play on a 2027 team).

Do you offer any discounts for bringing multiple teams from one lacrosse club?

Yes. Please call or text 281-608-9802.

Do you accept payment by check?

Yes. Please make checks payable to Hot Sauce Lacrosse and mail to P.O. Box 1234, Kingwood, Texas 77339.

What does it mean to be a “free agent” attending a tournament and how can I sign up as one?

When a player signs up as a free agent, she is signing up as an individual and will form a team of fellow free agent sign-ups or guest play on another team looking for members. The free agent will be coached by one of the Hot Sauce Lacrosse coaches, if playing for the House Team.

Do you require players to have a USA lacrosse membership?

Yes.

Cancellation/Refund policy

If weather or other unforeseen circumstances prevent an event from being completed or in some case, even starting, refunds will be as follows: 75% to teams that do not start any game and 50% to teams that compete 1 game. No refunds will be given to teams once they have completed 2 games.
